# Lights Out, Count

**USC CSCI426 Game Prototyping — Week 7 Prototype**

---

## About

You are a vampire. It is almost dawn. You need to get to your coffin.

The world is nearly pitch black, exactly how you like it. But the castle is full of **light**, and light is dangerous. Patches of it glow on the ground. Guards pace the halls carrying flashlights. Every second you spend in the light, you feel yourself fading.

Scattered across the castle are your **bedtime clothes** — your cap, your gown. Collect them all. Only then can you reach your coffin. Some lights will go out when you pick up a particular garment. Others strobe, waiting for you to time your crossing. Move quickly. The night is short.
